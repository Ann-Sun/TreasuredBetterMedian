Background

Members of 1 Million Woman are made up of women from all over the world who are committed to build a life style revolution to fight the climate crisis.

Research

Natalie Isaacs, the founder of 1MW (1 Million Women). Ten years ago, her lifestyle was different. she was a cosmetics manufacturer and her life is all about packaging. she thought climate change is an issue of someone else. But now she changes and devoted to the revolution of life style. There are many reasons for the change, but what is really connected in the end is that she reduceds her household electricity consumption by 20%. She said:

"When I saw that I saved a lot of money and carbon pollution, I realized that I was powerful. At that moment, I acquired ownership of this issue."

Everything we do will shape the world we want to live in. As individuals and collectives, we have tremendous power. This is the reason for 1MW's establishment and its purpose.



1MW convenes and encourages women to participate in activities including but not limited to climate-related activities, such as Walking for mother earth, love earth festival and Women power project. It also does reserch about women and climate like What women think about climate change.
